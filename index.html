BIO

Andrei Pancu is a contemporary artist residing and working in Ottawa, Canada. Born in 1968 in Bucharest, Romania, Pancu is known for combining chemically-induced patinas on ferrous metals with regular patterns to produce abstract compositions on a weathered surface. Layered, non-representational motifs allude at times to the organic, such as flora, fauna and geological strata, at others to architectural remnants and ancient symbolic structures. Forgoing narrative clarity for interpretive engagement, Pancu sets a warm, subdued and contemplative tone, evoking themes of antiquity and events suspended in time. The color palette is predominantly earthy, featuring deep browns, ochres, and muted reds.
